**Theorem** (Graham Pollak, 1971):
Let $D_n$ be the distance matrix of a tree, i.e.,

$$
(D_n)_{x,y} = d(x,y).
$$

Then

$$
\det(D_n) = (-1)^{n-1}(n-1)2^{n-2}.
$$
