I am a postdoc at the Department of Algebra, Faculty of Mathematics and Physics, Charles University, Prague, Czechia. I am working on the ERC Synergy project POCOCOP, supervised by Libor Barto.

Apr 2023 – Mar 2025: I was a postdoc at the Technical University of Denmark. I was working on a project, led by David Roberson, which relates quantum information theory and graph homomorphisms.

Apr 2022 – Mar 2023: I was a postdoc at the University of Neuchâtel, hosted by Alexander Kolpakov.

Oct 2016 – Feb 2022: I was a PhD student at the Department of Applied Mathematics, Faculty of Mathematics and Physics, Charles University.

Research interests:

  • Symmetries of discrete structures: graph isomorphism problem, automorphism groups of graphs, Weisfeiler-Leman dimension.
  • Interactions of quantum information theory, quantum groups, operator algebras, and graph theory. See Quantum Symmetry for an exposition of quantum spaces, quantum groups, and quantum symmetry.
