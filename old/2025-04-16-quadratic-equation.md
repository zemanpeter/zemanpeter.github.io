$$ax^2+bx+c = 0,$$

where $a\neq 0$.

$$
\begin{align*}
x^2 + \frac{b}{a}x + \frac{c}{a} &= 0\\
x\left(x + \frac{b}{a}x\right) + \frac{c}{a} &= 0\\
\end{align*}
$$
